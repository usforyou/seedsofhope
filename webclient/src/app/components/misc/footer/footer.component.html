About Seeds of Hope

Seeds of Hope is a effort aimed at equipping lower middle class to poor women with the knowledge, skills, and finances if necessary, to more efficiently manage, expand or start new businesses, as a way of getting their children off the street, to school, thereby saving a whole generation of children from the cycle of poverty and crime.
